    About me

    I am a human, like everyone else. However, I am lucky enough to be born as a Singaporean, in Singapore, with ready access to technology and the internet in my growing years. But I guess what is more interesting to you is that I have built numerous companies and sold two of them. At least 2 companies so far have crossed a million dollar in annual revenue. One of them has reached ~$10M in annual revenue.

    And I did them bootstrapped, as a broke solo founder. What I have instead of capital when I was starting out is an innumerable amount of failures and an absurd grit. For which I’d seek to document them down in this blog.

    This blog is dedicated to my children and their offspring, as I attempt to chronicle my expertise and experience in my wealth-building ventures. So hello, my darling children :)

    I grew up without a significant role model in my life. My mother tried her best in the sense that I was born in Singapore; I had clothes on me, food on the table, and a roof over my head, so I consider that a win. My father was absent and just a bad person through and through. I learned almost everything through books and by trying to mirror what society deems to be normal or well-adjusted. I don’t think my circumstances are unique. So, this goes out to everyone else who lacks proper parents but wants to learn. This is for you as well.
